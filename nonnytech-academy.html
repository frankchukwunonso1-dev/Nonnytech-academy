Do I need any coding experience to join?

Absolutely not. All three programmes are designed from the ground up for people with zero coding background. If you can use a smartphone, you can learn this.

How soon can I start earning after training?

Many students start approaching clients before they even finish the programme. By the time you graduate, you'll have a live portfolio and the pitch skills to close your first deal.

Are sessions online or in-person?

Both. In-person sessions run in Awka, Anambra. Online sessions are available for students across Nigeria and beyond. You choose what works for you.

What equipment do I need?

A laptop and a stable internet connection are ideal. If you only have a smartphone, contact us — we'll discuss what's possible for your situation.

How much does training cost?

Fees are kept affordable and vary by programme. Chat with us on WhatsApp to get the current fee structure — we'll be transparent about every cost upfront.

Will I get a certificate?

Yes — every graduate receives a Nonnytech Academy certificate of completion. More importantly, you'll have a live portfolio of real projects that speaks louder than any certificate.
